Regulation Title: Data Protection and Privacy Standards

Section 1: Purpose and Scope
1.1 Purpose:
The purpose of this regulation is to establish standards and practices to ensure the protection of personal data and the privacy of individuals in accordance with applicable laws.

1.2 Scope:
This regulation applies to all entities that process, store, or manage personal data, including employees, contractors, and third-party service providers.

Section 2: Data Processing Requirements
2.1 Data Collection:
Entities must collect personal data only for specified, explicit, and legitimate purposes, ensuring the data is relevant and limited to what is necessary.

2.2 Data Security:
Appropriate technical and organizational measures must be implemented to safeguard personal data against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

2.3 Data Retention:
Personal data must be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, in compliance with applicable retention schedules.

Section 3: Compliance and Enforcement
3.1 Monitoring:
Entities must implement regular audits and monitoring activities to ensure compliance with this regulation and address any non-conformities.

3.2 Violations:
Violations of this regulation may result in disciplinary actions, fines, or other penalties as stipulated by applicable law.

3.3 Reporting:
Any suspected or confirmed breaches of personal data must be reported within 72 hours to the designated data protection officer and relevant authorities.
